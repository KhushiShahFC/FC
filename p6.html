Project #6 Optical Illusion

Concept

In this project I chose to look at Indian designs and collect inspiration from henna designs. I recently went for my sisters wedding where we all applied henna that was very intricate and detailed. It had a lot of repitition so I tried to create something similar!

Reflection

At first I created three designs in Illustrator but I had an open mind for what the final result was supposed to look like. I played around with making different vertices to create an interesting but still Indian design using the begin shape/end shape function and added the loop I created to repeat. I would see this design being used on Indian sarees or Mehendi designs
