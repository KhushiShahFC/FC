Project #7 Data Portrait

Description

With this data portrait I show the number of times I say, "I don't know" during a day and track it for a whole week. it starts from Monday and Ends on Sunday.

Reflection

The idea came out of saying the phrase multiple times while doing coding assignments, I decided to track how much I say this as it is commonly spoken. If not for this exercise I wouldn't have gathered how much I do not know throughout the week. I first put in the days in text but decided to take it out to keep it more abstract.
