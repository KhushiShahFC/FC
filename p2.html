Project #2 Lost and Found

Design Process

This description of a lost and found object was given to me by Connor. He described his object as "I once lost my Stuffed Animal, It was a Webkinz elephant stuffed animal. It had two large ears,4 floppy legs and a chunky body. The color was overall Gray. The trunk has a slight curve on the bottom"

Reflections
